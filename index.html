FRC 2016 is here!

Yes, as part of the Saint Francis High School Robotics Team, I am now competing in the FRC 2016 Stronghold Competition!

For the next 6 weeks, Team 2367 (Saint Francis) will be be building a robot to compete in the Stronghold.

There are two aspects of the competition: the defenses and the tower.

The defenses are a series of obstacles that a robot has to drive over, while the tower needs to be taken down with foam boulders.

As of now, we have built a KOP chassis and we are working on a flywheel shooter mechanism.
